Curriculum Structure

Each academic program is composed of three main categories of subjects. Students must fulfill the credit requirements for all categories to be eligible for graduation.

1. Core Courses

These are compulsory major subjects that form the fundamental knowledge of your chosen field of study. The detailed list of core courses for each semester can be found in the GPA & Study Planner tab.

2. MPU / University Subjects

These are compulsory subjects mandated by the Ministry of Higher Education Malaysia. All students must pass these subjects. The specific requirements and subject options for Diploma and Bachelor programs are detailed below on this page.

3. Elective Courses

These courses allow students to explore specific areas of interest within their broader field. Students can choose from a predefined list of subjects to meet the elective credit requirements.

Tracking Graduation Progress

To graduate, a student must successfully complete all required credits from the three subject categories. Select a program below to inspect its credit distribution.
